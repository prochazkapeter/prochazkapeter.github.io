About me

Hello!

I graduated in June 2025 with a Master's degree in Open Informatics from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering at CTU in Prague, specializing in Computer Engineering. Throughout my studies, I focused on embedded systems—particularly the design of control software for platforms such as STM32 and ESP32—while also building a solid foundation in algorithms, parallel computing, and software efficiency. I gained hands-on experience with real-time operating systems (e.g., VxWorks) and became familiar with commonly used interfaces and protocols in the embedded development field.

Beyond IT, I have a strong interest in economics. During my studies, I completed courses in Management and Energy Security (led by Václav Bartuška, Special Envoy for Energy Security at the Czech Ministry of Foreign Affairs), and participated in a bilateral workshop with TU Wien focused on energy systems in the Czech Republic and Austria. This experience broadened my perspective on the intersection of technology, economics, and energy.
